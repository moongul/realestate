## Overview

EstateIntel is designed for professional real estate analytics. It uses a rigorous, institutional aesthetic that prioritizes readability and data clarity.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Authority:** Deep navy primary and serif headlines (`Source Serif 4`) convey stability and expertise.
- **Precision:** Tight spacing, sharp corners (`borderRadius.default: 2px`), and a clean grid.
- **Interactivity:** Professional blue secondary for actions and links.
- **Content-First:** Generous line-height for body text (`body-lg`) to facilitate reading long-form analysis.
